Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil water untile al dente.
  3. Add cream and stir over low heat.
  4. Drain pasta and mix into sauce.
  5. season with salt, pepper, and parsley. serve hot!
